Drag and drop images from your computer or internet to the empty tiles, then press Start!

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same image touch, they merge into one!

Background story: Everybody in the office is playing 2048. One fine day somebody floated the idea of making a version of 2048 where you can upload your own images to be used as the tiles. So here it is.

Note: This is NOT the official version of 2048. You can play the original version here.

Created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
Modified by Irwan to allow users to upload their own images to be used as the tiles.
